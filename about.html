    • Welcome to our restaurant, located in the heart of Palam Colony, New Delhi, where we've been serving our customers with love and passion for over 20 years. Our restaurant offers a unique blend of South Indian and Chinese cuisine, ensuring that every visit is a delightful culinary journey. Whether you're in the mood for the rich flavors of dosa, idli, and sambar, or crave the tangy, spicy tastes of Chinese delicacies like noodles, Manchurian, and fried rice, we have something for everyone.


    Founded by Mr. Surjan Saini, a visionary with a passion for food, our restaurant has grown into one of the most popular dining destinations in the area. Mr. Saini's dedication to quality, taste, and customer satisfaction has made us a favorite among locals and visitors alike. We pride ourselves on our warm hospitality, cozy atmosphere, and commitment to serving dishes that are both authentic and freshly prepared.


    We are also proud to offer Zomato delivery services, making it easier than ever for our customers to enjoy our delicious food from the comfort of their homes. Our restaurant has become a well-known landmark in the community, surrounded by several popular dining spots, yet we continue to stand out due to our unwavering dedication to excellence.


    Whether you're looking for a quick meal, a family dinner, or a place to celebrate a special occasion, we invite you to experience the flavors, aromas, and hospitality that have made us a trusted name in Palam Colony for the last two decades.

    Ravi Saini, the owner our restaurant, is a passionate entrepreneur with a deep love for food. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, Ravi has dedicated himself to creating a dining space that offers both authenticity and variety. His vision of blending South Indian and Chinese cuisines has made the restaurant a popular spot in Palam Colony, New Delhi. Under his leadership, the restaurant has flourished, gaining a loyal customer base and becoming a go-to destination for food lovers in the area.
